Complete reverse osmosis water treatment system designed for industrial food processing facility in Dar es Salaam, providing clean, safe water for production.

Industries

Date

RO Systems Pre-treatment Industrial Food Grade

Tanzania Food Processing Ltd faced critical water quality issues affecting their production line and product safety standards.

The existing water supply contained high levels of dissolved salts, bacteria, and organic compounds that compromised product quality and equipment longevity. They needed a reliable, food-grade water treatment solution that could provide consistent quality while meeting international food safety standards.

Kilitek Africa designed and installed a comprehensive 5,000 LPH reverse osmosis system with multi-stage pre-treatment including sediment filtration, activated carbon, and water softening to ensure optimal RO membrane performance.

The system includes automated monitoring, CIP (Clean-in-Place) capabilities, and redundant pumps for continuous operation. All components meet food-grade standards with stainless steel construction and FDA-approved materials throughout the water contact surfaces.

Working with Kilitek Africa was exceptional! They understood our critical water quality requirements and delivered a system that exceeded our expectations. The installation was professional, on-time, and their ongoing support has been outstanding. Our production quality has improved significantly, and we've achieved full compliance with international food safety standards.

James Mwalimu

Production Manager at Tanzania Food Processing Ltd
